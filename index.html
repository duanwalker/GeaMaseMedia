About Gea Mase Media

Gea Mase Media only uses the very latest and finest digital technology on every job for flawless audio experience for clients and the audience alike! Mixing on a wireless iPad allows our engineer to be anywhere in the room totally out of the way and ensuring complete and even coverage in each and every venue. Able to provide audio reinforcement for venues indoor and outdoor ranging from intimate settings with very light production to auditoriums seating thousands complete with lights and backline!

Whether looking to record a demo, EP, CD or voice over, GMM has the gear, knowledge and experience to ensure your product will be of the highest quality and with a 100% client satisfaction guarantee AND track record, it’s assured you will be well pleased with your product.

Our sound engineers have years of combined experience and GMM has a team that is deep enough to run multiple shows simultaneously! When you’re in need of quality sound reinforcement, lighting services or just an engineer to mix on your system that’s already in place, GMM has you covered with ease!!
